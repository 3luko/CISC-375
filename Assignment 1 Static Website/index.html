Drake

Rapper, Singer, Songwriter & Cultural Icon

About Drake

Aubrey Drake Graham, born October 24, 1986, in Toronto, Canada, is one of the best-selling music artists of all time with over 170 million records sold worldwide.

Drake first gained recognition as an actor on the Canadian teen drama Degrassi: The Next Generation before transitioning to music. He signed with Lil Wayne's Young Money Entertainment in 2009 after his mixtape So Far Gone went viral online.

He is widely credited for popularizing Toronto in hip-hop culture, often referring to the city as "the 6." Drake is known for blending rapping and singing in the same song, a style that influenced a large number of artists who came after him.

Drake co-founded OVO Sound in 2012, his own record label and lifestyle brand. He has held the record for most Billboard Hot 100 entries by a solo artist and has topped the Billboard 200 with every studio album he has released.

Career Timeline

  1. 2001

    Joins Degrassi: The Next Generation

    At 15, Drake is cast as Jimmy Brooks on the popular Canadian teen drama, a role he held for eight seasons.

  2. 2009

    Releases So Far Gone — Signs with Young Money

    "Best I Ever Had" reaches #2 on the Billboard Hot 100. Lil Wayne signs Drake to Young Money Entertainment.

  3. 2010

    Debut Album: Thank Me Later

    Debuts at #1 on the Billboard 200, selling 447,000 copies in its first week.

  4. 2011

    Take Care — Grammy Award Winner

    His second studio album wins the Grammy for Best Rap Album and is widely considered his best work.

  5. 2012

    OVO Sound Label Founded

    Drake co-founds OVO Sound with producer Noah "40" Shebib, expanding his brand into a full label and lifestyle company.

  6. 2016

    Views Hits 1 Billion Streams

    First album to reach 1 billion Apple Music streams. "One Dance" becomes Drake's first US #1 single.

  7. 2018

    Scorpion Breaks Streaming Records

    Places 7 songs simultaneously in the Billboard Hot 100 top 10 — a record for a solo artist at the time.

  8. 2023

    For All the Dogs Released

    Drake's eighth studio album debuts at #1, continuing his unbroken streak of chart-topping projects.
