Notable Singles

Some of Drake's most successful and well-known singles include:

  • Best I Ever Had (2009) — #2 Billboard Hot 100
  • Take Care ft. Rihanna (2011) — Grammy Award winner
  • Started From the Bottom (2013) — Cultural anthem
  • One Dance (2016) — Drake's first US #1 single
  • God's Plan (2018) — #1 for 11 weeks
  • Toosie Slide (2020) — #1 US
  • Rich Flex with 21 Savage (2022) — #1 US
